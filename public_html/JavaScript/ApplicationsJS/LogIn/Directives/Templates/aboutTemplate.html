Welcome to NooLib Web Application

Because, everyday, numerous scientific algorithms are created by students, engineers and researchers all over the world. Because, nowaday, it seems complicated to promote them and keep them updated for being finally abandoned. For these reasons, we have invented NooLib.

NooLib is a web application which enables to host and promote your own algorithms for Research. With NooLib, you can easily deposit your own programs and transform them into an useful application. Then you can share them with people and interact smartly with other applications present on NooLib.

With Noolib, we would like to create a better place for improving your researches efficiency and help you to promote your work.

We hope sincerely you will be satisfied by this novel concept and we remain at your disposal for any suggestions which could improve your experience in NooLib.
